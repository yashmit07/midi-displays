About

This web application creates visual representations of MIDI input. Connect a MIDI device to see colorful visualizations respond to your playing.

Requirements

  • A modern web browser that supports WebMIDI (Chrome or Edge recommended)
  • A connected MIDI device

Note Ranges

  • Lower range: Notes 72-83 (C4-B4)
  • Middle range: Notes 84-95 (C5-B5)
  • Upper range: Notes 96+ (C6 and above)
